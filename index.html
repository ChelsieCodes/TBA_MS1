Who is The Bark Academy?

At The Bark Academy, we're passionate about helping dogs and their owners build strong, lasting bonds through positive reinforcement and effective training techniques. Founded on the belief that every dog deserves a happy, well-behaved life, our academy offers a variety of classes tailored to fit the unique needs of each dog, from playful puppies to experienced canines.

Our Mission: Our mission is to create a supportive and encouraging environment where dogs and their owners can thrive together. We believe that training should be fun, rewarding, and stress-free for both you and your dog. Through our comprehensive programs, we aim to enhance the relationship between you and your dog, ensuring that your furry friend becomes a well-mannered, confident, and happy companion.

What We Offer: We offer a wide range of classes, from basic obedience and puppy socialization to advanced agility and specialized courses like therapy dog preparation and scent work. Whether you're looking to teach your dog essential manners, engage in fun activities like tricks and rally obedience, or prepare for competitive sports, we have a class for you.

Our Team: Our certified trainers are experienced, compassionate, and dedicated to using positive reinforcement methods that are both effective and humane. With years of experience in dog behaviour, training, and care, our team is committed to providing the best possible education for both dogs and their owners.

Why choose The Bark Academy?

Behaviour

Our primary focus is on understanding and addressing behavioral issues. We work with dogs to resolve problems such as aggression, anxiety, and excessive barking, ensuring they become well-adjusted members of your family.

Obedience

From basic commands like sit and stay to advanced obedience skills, we teach dogs to follow instructions reliably. Our goal is to create a foundation of good manners that can be built upon as your dog matures.

Socialisation

Proper socialisation is crucial for a well-rounded dog. We provide controlled environments where dogs can safely interact with other dogs and people, helping them develop confidence and positive social behaviours.

Owner Education

Training is as much about educating owners as it is about training dogs. We equip you with the knowledge and tools needed to reinforce good behavior at home, ensuring lasting results and a strong, positive relationship with your dog.
