Why choose The Bark Academy?

Behaviour

Our primary focus is on understanding and addressing behavioral issues. We work with dogs to resolve problems such as aggression, anxiety, and excessive barking, ensuring they become well-adjusted members of your family.

Obedience

From basic commands like sit and stay to advanced obedience skills, we teach dogs to follow instructions reliably. Our goal is to create a foundation of good manners that can be built upon as your dog matures.

Socialisation

Proper socialisation is crucial for a well-rounded dog. We provide controlled environments where dogs can safely interact with other dogs and people, helping them develop confidence and positive social behaviours.

Owner Education

Training is as much about educating owners as it is about training dogs. We equip you with the knowledge and tools needed to reinforce good behavior at home, ensuring lasting results and a strong, positive relationship with your dog.
